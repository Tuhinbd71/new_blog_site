Travel in Bangladesh: A complete travel guide for Bangladesh in 2024.

Bangladesh is a beautiful country with a rich cultural heritage and diverse natural beauty. From bustling cities to serene beaches, and from ancient temples to tea gardens, Bangladesh has something to offer for every traveler. In this travel guide, we will cover everything you need to know about traveling to Bangladesh in 2024. Visa Requirements: Most foreign nationals require a visa to enter Bangladesh. You can obtain a visa from a Bangladeshi embassy or consulate in your home country. Alternatively, you can apply for an e-Visa online through the official website of the Bangladesh government. Best Time to Visit: The best time to visit Bangladesh is from October to March when the weather is pleasant and comfortable. The monsoon season runs from June to September, which can cause flooding and landslides.

Best time of year to visit Bangladesh

The best time visit Bangladesh is during the ‘cool season’ (effectively considered winter in Bangladesh) from November to February. Days are warm averaging a comfortable 25°C and nights are cooler. From March onwards the days get rapidly hotter and monsoon generally arrives sometime in June.
